Jackson Warrior Restoration

It was in terrible shape when I received it after many years of abuse.

After disassembly, I filled and sanded the imperfections in the finish.

The color in the edges where it was dented was lost during the filling process so I had to respray parts of the burst. I also re-sprayed clear on the entire body and went through the wet sanding and buffing process.

All of the individual metal parts were derusted and refinished. The ones that were too far gone were replaced.
