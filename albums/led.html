RGB LED Fretboard

In 2017 I saw Steve Vai live in Puerto Rico and at the very start of the show in pitch black darkness, he walked out with a guitar I had never seen before, "Bo". It's a custom mirror Ibanez Jem with LEDs on the fretboard. That image has not escaped my mind since and I was lucky enough to hold that very same guitar in 2022 while participating in the Steve Vai EVO experience. I've always wanted to try to replicate that somehow but make it even better if I could. That's how this project started.

I came up with a system that I am nicknaming the "Photon" system. It is a pedal controlled fretboard that is capable of over 16 million colors and each LED is individually addressable, allowing individual color and brightness levels. The pedal can be reprogrammed, allowing settings for color changes and even animations.
